Welcome to elderHelper!

As a non-profit collaboration team, we are here to bring you the next best tech solution to your elderly health needs.

The main purpose of this application is to help the elderly get the medical help or care they need

You can log-in as a helper or a patient

While logged in as a Helper, you can see a list of current, active patients within 25 miles of your Zip Code. From here, you can see patient details, and any comments they may have left. Then, simply reach out by phone to offer some help.

While logged in as a Patient, we will simply ask a short servey to see how you feel. Once you're done with that, you can sit back, relax, and wait for a reply from a nearby Helper.
